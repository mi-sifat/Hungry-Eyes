Who We Are

Taste the Difference

Hungry Eyes Food Corner has been serving the people of Mirpur, Dhaka with fresh, delicious, and 100% Halal food since 2017. We believe every meal should be a memorable experience — crafted with the finest ingredients, prepared with passion, and served with love.

From our crispy pizzas to juicy burgers, soul-warming soups, and refreshing drinks — every dish at Hungry Eyes is made to satisfy your cravings and keep you coming back for more.

With 4 branches across Mirpur and surrounding areas, we are proud to be one of Dhaka's trusted Halal food destinations. Whether you dine in, take away, or order through our delivery partners — the Hungry Eyes experience is always the same: outstanding food at honest prices.
